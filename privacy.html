Privacy Policy (Plain Language)

What this app does

Next Bill helps you run an on-device SAFE/NOT SAFE check using your balances, due dates, and optional statement CSV imports.

What data is collected

  • Data you enter (balances, due dates, rent, buffer floor).
  • Optional CSV statement data you import.
  • Saved profile settings and mapping rules.

Where your data goes

  • Your data stays on your device.
  • No accounts, no bank connections, no OCR, no AI processing.
  • No analytics and no third-party scripts are loaded by this app.
  • Data is stored locally using browser storage (IndexedDB / local storage).
